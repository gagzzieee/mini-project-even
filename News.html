News

Copenhagen Photo Festival is the largest photo festival in the Nordic region, running on June 3-30, 2021, with more than 50 exhibitions in collaboration with galleries, museums and cultural institutions in Copenhagen and southern Sweden. Each year, 250 Danish and foreign photographers contribute, and the festival is visited by around 55.000 people. In June, photography is on the agenda under the three main pillars Framing Identity, Framing Society and Framing Vision, through exhibitions, workshops, talks, portfolio reviews and more.

Get Ready For Futures Nordiac Talents.

Copenhagen Photo Festival has been invited as a partner for the 4th edition of the FUTURES, a platform co-funded by the Creative Europe Programme of the European Union focusing on amplifying emerging talents in photography in Europe. Since 2017, FUTURES has been offering a full programme to increase the capacity, mobility and visibility to various artists selected every year by leading art institutions across Europe. As a new FUTURES partner, Copenhagen Photo Festival has created its new programme FUTURES NORDIC TALENTS. The programme will start in January 2021, with a one-month open call for upcoming and recent graduates of art or documentary photography with Nordic residency. .

Good bye, Europe

Since 2018, Copenhagen Photo Festival has been part of the Creative Europe co-funded project Reconstruction of Identities together with three other partners, all working within the field of photography and visual culture. The project reflected the cultural diversity among different countries in Europe and was finalized this fall through the last exhibition of four. Throughout the year each exhibition has been presented in each of the four partners’ respective countries: NOOR Foundation in the Netherlands, Copenhagen Photo Festival in Denmark, Ad Hoc Gestion Cultural in Spain and the Municipality of Savignano sul Rubicone – SIFEST in Italy.

Many thanks to the City of Copenhagen

Many thanks to the City of Copenhagen, who also in 2021 supports the realization of Copenhagen Photo Festival. With their support, we can continue to experiment with exhibition format and involve both Copenhagen and the Copenhageners when the festival returns on June 3-13 2021.

We are looking for interns for Winters 2021

COPENHAGEN PHOTO FESTIVALS vision is to strengthen our ability to interpret the photography as an artform. We work the whole year to convey and set in motion initiatives about the photography with the festival being the high point of the year. In 2021 the festival will run from the 3rd-13th of June, and we are now looking for interns, who can offer professionalism, opinions and excitement! We are a small organisation with an informal work environment, located in Villa Kultur on Østerbro. As an intern you will have influence on the realization of a comprehensive culture event with a big number og collaborators and a broad range of activities. During your internship you will get experience with both event- and exhibition work, and you will be taught to be in charge of independent projects going foward. You will put your academic knowledge and education to use with assignments in a practical and creative context. You get a broad insight into the photo- and artworld as well as the cultural environment on an international level.
